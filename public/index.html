Directions for use

  • Use undiluted for rapid hand antisepsis
  • Apply to clean & dry hands
  • Rub in hands & fingernails with product
  • Rub vigorously over all surfaces & allow hands to dry
  • Rub 3ml in hands for 30 seconds for Hygienic Hand Disinfection
  • Rub 6ml in hands for 90 seconds for Surgical Hand Disinfection
  • For children under 6, use only under adult supervision
  • Not recommended for infants

Precautions

  • For external use only
  • Flammable, keep away from fire or flame
  • Keep away from children
  • Avoid direct contact with eyes
  • Do not store above 40ºC
  • May discolor some fabrics and may be harmful to wood finishes and plastics
